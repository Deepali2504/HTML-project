RECIPE

Muddle pomegranate pearls/ anar
10 mint leaves
1/2 cup crushed ice
For shikanji masala powder:
2 tsp Bhuna jeera powder
2 tsp saunf powder
2 tsp chaat masala powder
1 tsp black salt
- mix well and add 1 tsp to the serving glass
1/2 lemon juice
1-2 tsp sugar powder/ sweetener of choice
Top with chilled water
For garnish:
Lemon slice
Pomegranate pearls

POMEGRANATE SHIKANJI

Ingredients

  • Pomegranate pearls
  • Sugar
  • Lemon
  • Jeera powder
  • Black salt
  • Mint leaves
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest, or to taste
